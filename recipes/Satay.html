Satay

Description

Satay is a beloved Indonesian street food consisting of skewered and grilled meat, typically served with a rich peanut sauce. Smoky, sweet, and savory, it's a staple at gatherings and roadside stalls across the country.

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken thigh, cubed
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water
  • 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

For the peanut sauce

  • 200 g roasted peanuts, ground
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 red chilies
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 200 ml water
  • Salt and sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken cubes with sweet soy sauce and oil for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Thread the marinated chicken onto the soaked skewers.
  3. Grill the skewers over medium heat, turning occasionally, until cooked and lightly charred.
  4. For the sauce, blend garlic and chilies, then sauté briefly until fragrant.
  5. Add ground peanuts, water, sweet soy sauce, salt, and sugar; simmer until thickened.
  6. Serve the grilled skewers drizzled with peanut sauce.
