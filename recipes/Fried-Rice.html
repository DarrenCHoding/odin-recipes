Fried Rice

Description

Nasi Goreng is Indonesia's iconic fried rice dish, stir-fried with a savory blend of sweet soy sauce, garlic, and shallots, often topped with a fried egg and crispy shallots. It's a quick, comforting meal enjoyed any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 shallots, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 red chilies, sliced
  • 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Sliced cucumber and tomato, for garnish
  • Fried shallots, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok and sauté shallots, garlic, and chilies until fragrant.
  2. Push the aromatics aside, crack in the eggs, and scramble until just set.
  3. Add the cooked rice, breaking up any clumps.
  4. Pour in the sweet soy sauce and stir-fry until everything is evenly coated and heated through.
  5. Season with salt to taste.
  6. Serve garnished with cucumber, tomato, and fried shallots.
